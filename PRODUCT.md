# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Small to medium-sized businesses and startups looking for custom software development. Both domestic Chinese clients and international clients. They need technical partners who can deliver websites, apps, systems, mini-programs, AI solutions, and e-commerce platforms.

## Product Purpose

Zhiwei (知微) is a boutique development studio that builds digital products. The website showcases our capabilities, past work, and team to attract new clients. Success = qualified inbound leads from clients who trust our technical expertise.

## Brand Personality

- **Expert but approachable** - Deep technical skill without corporate coldness
- **Creative yet reliable** - Innovative solutions with solid execution
- **Global mindset** - Bilingual, culturally aware, serving both local and international markets

Voice: Professional but warm. Technical confidence without jargon overload.

## Anti-references

- Generic SaaS landing pages with purple gradients and three equal feature cards
- Corporate agency sites that feel soulless and templated
- Overly playful dev portfolios that prioritize style over substance
- Sites that use "empower", "transform", "leverage", "cutting-edge" unironically

## Design Principles

1. **Show, don't tell** - Let the work speak. Real project screenshots over abstract illustrations.
2. **Practice what we preach** - As a dev studio, our own site must demonstrate technical and design excellence.
3. **Bilingual-first** - Not an afterthought. Both languages get equal design attention.
4. **Intentional motion** - Every animation communicates something (hierarchy, storytelling, feedback). No decoration loops.
5. **Confident restraint** - Bold where it matters (typography, hero), quiet where it serves (body copy, secondary sections).

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- WCAG AA minimum compliance
- Respect `prefers-reduced-motion` - all animations degrade gracefully
- Semantic HTML structure
- Keyboard navigable
- Sufficient color contrast (4.5:1 body, 3:1 large text)
